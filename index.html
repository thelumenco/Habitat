Installing as Mobile App
For the best experience, add Habitat to your home screen. On Chrome (Android): Tap the three dots (â‹®) at top right, then "Add to Home screen". On Safari (iPhone): Tap the Share button, scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen". The app will open like a native app without browser bars.
Envisioning Your Future Self
The Vision tab helps you visualize who you want to become in 6 months. This gives context and meaning to the habits you're buildingâ€”each action becomes a step toward that future self. If you're using this app for something other than personal health habits (like household routines, for example), you can toggle off the Vision tab in Settings if you don't need it. Note: The preset habits that come with the app are generic, so customize them to align with your personal vision. Add, remove, and rename categories and actions to match your unique goals.
Personalizing Your Habitat
Click on your name in the header (it says "Friend" by default) to customize it. Type your name and press Enter to save it.
Data Storage and Privacy
Your data is automatically saved to your browser on this device and remains completely private. All information is stored locally on your device onlyâ€”nothing is sent to any server or cloud. Your data does not sync across devices or browsers. To use Habitat on another device, export your data and import it on the other device. Warning: Clearing your browser cache or data will erase all your progress. Export your data regularly as a backup.
Earning Points
Tap any action button to log it and earn points. Use the "Log Steps" field to enter custom step counts (1 point per 500 steps).
Customizing Categories
Click any category name (like "Workouts" or "Nutrition") to rename it. Click "+ Add" in any category to create custom actions with your own emoji, name, and point values. You can also add new categories using the "+ Add New Category" button at the bottom of the Earn tab, and delete custom categories using the trash icon in the category header.
Managing Rewards
In the Rewards tab, click "+ Add" to create custom rewards with an emoji, name, and point cost. When you have enough points, click "Redeem" to unlock a reward. Use "Undo" if you change your mind.
Managing Items
Swipe left on any preset, reward, or history item to reveal the delete button. Swipe right on presets and rewards to edit them (change emoji, name, and points/cost). You can also drag and drop presets to reorder them within a category or move them between categories. Note: deleting a history item will subtract those points from your total.
Spin the Logo for a Surprise
Tap or swipe the hexagon logo next to "HABITAT" for a surprise! You'll get either a motivational quote or a bonus.
How Bonuses Work
When you spin the logo, you have about a 10% chance of receiving a bonus! Instant bonuses (+3 or +5 points) are added immediately. Multiplier bonuses (2x or 3x) activate for your next action onlyâ€”a golden badge will appear below your name. Once you log an action with a multiplier, it's consumed and disappears. Important: If you delete an action that used a bonus, you won't get the bonus backâ€”bonuses are one-time use only.
Export & Import Data
Export your data to save a backup as a JSON file. Import allows you to restore from a previously exported file. This is useful for transferring data between devices or keeping backups.
Habit Accumulation Tracker
HABITAT stands for Habit Accumulation Trackerâ€”a tool designed to help you build positive routines through consistent action and earned rewards.
A Natural Environment for Growth
The name also alludes to the idea of a habitat being a natural environment. Rather than feeling forced, your habits are designed into your environment, making positive actions feel organic and sustainable.
An Ecosystem of Positive Actions
HABITAT works twofold: it helps you both build habits and hold off on impulse decisions by positioning them as rewards that you earn permission for. This creates an ecosystem where every action contributes to your growth, and indulgences become intentional celebrations of your progress.
Works with "No Zero Days"
HABITAT pairs perfectly with the "No Zero Days" approach to habit-building. Create actions for min, medium, and max levels of each habit and assign points accordingly. For example: "10 min workout" (5 pts), "30 min workout" (15 pts), "Full gym session" (25 pts). This way, doing something is always better than nothing, and you're rewarded proportionally for your effort.
